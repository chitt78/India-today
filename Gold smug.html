Who is Ranya Rao, Kannada actor arrested in Bengaluru for smuggling gold.

Kannada actor Ranya Rao was arrested for smuggling 14.8 kg of gold worth over Rs 12 crore at Bengaluru airport. She has acted in two Kannada films and a Tamil film.

In short
*Kannada actor Ranya Rao was arrested on gold smuggling charges on March 3
*She is the stepdaughter of IPS officer Ramachandra Rao
*She has done three films in Kannada and Tamil film industries

Kannada actor Ranya Rao was arrested for allegedly attempting to smuggle 14.8 kg of gold worth over Rs 12 crore. She was held at the Bengaluru International airport on March 3. Ranya is a Kannada and Tamil actor, who has three films to her credit. She is the stepdaughter of IPS officer Ramachandra Rao, who currently serves as the Director General of Police (DGP) at the Karnataka State Police Housing Corporation.
