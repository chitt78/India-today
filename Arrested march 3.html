The Directorate of Revenue Intelligence (DRI) raided the residence of Kannada actor Ranya Rao in Bengaluru after arresting her for smuggling 14.2 kg of gold worth Rs 12.56 crore through Kempegowda International Airport. The search at her Lavelle Road home led to the recovery of gold jewellery worth Rs 2.06 crore and Rs 2.67 crore in cash. Three large boxes were also confiscated during the search, bringing the total seizure value to Rs 17.29 crore, making it one of the biggest gold smuggling cases at Bengaluru airport in recent times, as per the DRI.
