Ranya Rao's father ordered protocol assistance at airport: Cop's damning claim

Constable Basavaraj claimed DGP Ramachandra Rao, Ranya Rao's stepfather, instructed him to provide protocol assistance at Bengaluru Airport. Basavaraj denied involvement in gold smuggling but admitted assisting her multiple times.

In short
*Constable says actor’s DGP father ordered protocol help for Ranya Rao
*Basavaraj denies knowledge of Ranya’s alleged gold smuggling
*Says he knew Ranya for many years, assisted her 3-4 times before

The protocol officer who assisted Kannada actor Ranya Rao, arrested for smuggling gold, claimed that Karnataka DGP Ramachandra Rao, the actor’s stepfather, had specifically instructed him to provide assistance to her at Bengaluru’s Kempegowda International Airport. Basavaraj, a constable stationed at the Kempegowda Airport Police Station, revealed that he was following direct orders from Ramachandra Rao. His duties involved ensuring smooth arrivals and departures for Ranya Rao based on instructions from senior officials.
